News Editor

I am a senior at Carmel High School who is fascinated by the world of journalism. In my first year on HiLite staff I was a Feature Reporter and Cover Story Copy Editor. For my second year on HiLite Staff I was the News Section Co-Editor. This year I was a Managing Editor of the HiLite publication. Outside of the HiLite I enjoy leading a variety of journalism initiatives, including leading middle school journalistic writing workshops for the North South Foundation.
